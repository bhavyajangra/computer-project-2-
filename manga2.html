Timeless Manga Classics

Astro Boy

1952-1968

The story of a robot boy that helped establish manga's visual style.

Impact: Pioneer of modern manga and anime

Phoenix

1967-1988

Osamu Tezuka's philosophical masterpiece about life, death, and reincarnation.

Impact: Elevated manga as an art form

Devilman

1972-1973

A dark series that influenced the horror and supernatural genre.

Impact: Defined dark fantasy manga

Galaxy Express 999

1977-1981

A space opera about a boy's journey through the galaxy.

Impact: Revolutionized sci-fi manga

Fist of the North Star

1983-1988

A post-apocalyptic martial arts series that defined the genre.

Impact: Influenced action manga

Akira

1982-1990

A cyberpunk masterpiece that gained worldwide recognition.

Impact: Global manga breakthrough
