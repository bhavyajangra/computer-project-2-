Legendary Mangaka

Osamu Tezuka

1928-1989

"The God of Manga" who revolutionized the medium and created over 700 manga series.

Notable Works: Astro Boy, Black Jack, Phoenix

Eiichiro Oda

Born: 1975

Creator of the best-selling manga series of all time, known for intricate world-building.

Notable Works: One Piece

Naoki Urasawa

Born: 1960

Master of mystery and suspense, known for complex psychological narratives.

Notable Works: Monster, 20th Century Boys, Pluto
