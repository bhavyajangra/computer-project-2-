Leading Animation Studios

Studio Ghibli

Founded: 1985

Known for their masterful storytelling and beautiful hand-drawn animation.

Notable Works: Spirited Away, My Neighbor Totoro, Princess Mononoke

MAPPA

Founded: 2011

Modern powerhouse known for high-quality action sequences and dynamic animation.

Notable Works: Attack on Titan Final Season, Jujutsu Kaisen, Chainsaw Man

Kyoto Animation

Founded: 1981

Renowned for their attention to detail and emotional storytelling.

Notable Works: Violet Evergarden, A Silent Voice, K-On!

ufotable

Founded: 2000

Masters of combining 2D animation with digital effects.

Notable Works: Demon Slayer, Fate Series, Garden of Sinners

Bones

Founded: 1998

Known for their high-quality action scenes and original productions.

Notable Works: My Hero Academia, Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood, Mob Psycho 100

Wit Studio

Founded: 2012

Recognized for their dynamic action sequences and unique art style.

Notable Works: Attack on Titan (S1-S3), Vinland Saga, Spy x Family
